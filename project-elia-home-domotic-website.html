What is Elia Home ?

Depending on the user's lifestyle and the seasons, the AI will learn and automate the home. Example: It is important to know that not all domestic appliances are really turned off. The TV: When the user turns off the TV with the remote control, the AI will intervene to cut off the power completely, to save little by little the consumption. This is valid for any type of device connected to the sockets. All its data will be transmitted on the screen via a menu displaying the live temperature of the home, the electricity consumption per day/week/month in KwH and will propose to display the energy saving of the home per day/week/month with an automatic conversion of the Kwh in euros to realize the blow save.
